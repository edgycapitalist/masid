2. **Database Migration**: Database migration can be complex and time-consuming. Mitigation: Assign a dedicated DBA for database migration, and conduct thorough testing and validation.
3. **Dependency Issues**: Microservices dependencies can cause issues during migration. Mitigation: Conduct a thorough assessment of dependencies, and migrate microservices in a logical order.
4. **Security**: Ensuring SOC 2 compliance throughout the migration process poses a risk. Mitigation: Assign a dedicated security engineer to ensure compliance, and conduct regular security audits.
5. **Budget Overrun**: The budget constraint poses a risk of cost overrun. Mitigation: Monitor expenses closely, prioritize tasks, and allocate resources efficiently.
