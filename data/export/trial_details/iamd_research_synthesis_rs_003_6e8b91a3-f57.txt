Hoffmann et al. (2022) argued that most large language models are undertrained relative to their parameter count. The Chinchilla paper suggested that:
1. **Undertraining**: Models with a large number of parameters may not be trained for a sufficient number of steps, leading to underutilization of their capacity (Hoffmann et al., 2022).
